• The form is the canonical neumorphic context. Every input control uses neumorphic depth as a semantic signal: inset = "you write into this"; raised = "you press this".

• Name + textarea: inset wells (深陷的). The text appears "set into" the surface.
• Quantity stepper: inset input + two raised round buttons (the entire stepper row composes 3 different surface depths).
• Price-type radio: inset track + raised "selected" pill. Three options.
• Bring-condition toggle: same vocabulary, two options (会带去 / 需自取).
• Form intro line "愿你的多余，刚好是别人想要的" in STKaiti — single accent moment, 1 event.
